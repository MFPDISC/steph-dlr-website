Product Bio

The Ultimate tool
For Joint Mobility
& Athletic Flow

Rope flowing isn't just a trend — it's an essential training method for elite combat athletes. By combining rhythm-based mobility with light resistance, the Steph DLR Flow Rope helps you develop active shoulder health, seamless hand-eye coordination, and fluid fighting footwork.

Built from heavyweight, high-density double-braided nylon, this rope is specifically weighted at 450g to provide maximum momentum and structural feedback, enabling you to feel the drag and coordinate your body seamlessly.

🛡️

Active Joint Recovery

Decompresses and mobilizes shoulders, elbows, and wrists. Excellent for rehab, warmups, and flushing out tightness.

⚡

Fighter Coordination

Teaches the upper body and feet to work in perfect synchronicity, directly improving ring movement, timing, and strike evasion.

🫁

Low-Impact Cardio

Elevates heart rate and improves cardiovascular endurance without the joint-jarring impact of roadwork.

🧠

Mental Flow State

Builds deep focus and spatial awareness through meditative, rhythmic movement. Perfect for calming the nervous system.
